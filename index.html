    PLATE III.

    The Scrivener's Desk

    in which the rites and tools may be amended
    · · ·
    i. Of Appearance
    palette of the page the colour by which thy field is set
    stature of the letter the height to which thy glyphs may rise
    24px
    gliding caret that the cursor flow as ink upon glass
    declare the pace as it runs a tally kept whilst the glass is yet running
    ii. Of Practice
    voice of the quill the report of the keystroke against vellum
    strength of the voice how loudly the quill speaks
    30%
    music of the desk a soft procedural lounge, played low beneath the trial
    level of the music the music's own volume, set apart from the quill
    50%
    stay upon thine error no advance until the mistake be corrected
    confidence of the hand no retreat: the backspace is forbidden
    blind passage errors made known only upon conclusion
    at the ready a three-count before each trial begins
    the ghost tracer a translucent rival of thy best run, paced word by word — in any manner of trial
    ambient atmosphere stars, leaves, tides, incense, aurora — the per-palette mood
    depth of atmosphere how loudly the room speaks — set softer or louder to thy taste
    60%
    iii. Of Affliction — honest burdens, undertaken at thy choosing
    the mist words yet to come fade into vapour at the page's edge
    the fading ink that which is past dims behind thee, as ink upon old vellum
    the dim lantern the room is darker; the page lit only by a guttering wick
    the narrow eye the field is veiled at its edges — only the centre remains plain
    the cramped hand the letters are set tight upon the line, crowding the eye
    the guttering candle the lantern light flickers and gutters as if caught in a draught
    the foxed page age-stains bloom across the vellum, mottling the field
    the heavy air the ink wans, its contrast washed thin as through a haze
    iv. Of the Vault — that thy ledger may be sealed and borne abroad
    seal the ledger cast all trials, settings, streaks, and seals into a single archive — saved to thy disk
    unseal a ledger restore from a sealed archive — replaces all present records, with thy leave
    ✦
